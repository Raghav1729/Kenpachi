# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes our commitment to your privacy when you use the Kenpachi application ("App"). We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. We are committed to protecting your privacy by not collecting any data at all.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1. No Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information, device information, usage data, or any other information from users. The App does not track, store, or transmit any user data.

### 2.2. No Automatic Data Collection
The App does not automatically collect any information including device information, usage data, or technical information.

### 2.3. No Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party services that collect information. We do not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or any other third-party data collection services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is no user data to use. The App functions entirely locally on your device without transmitting any data.

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information with outside parties. Since no data is collected, there is no information to share under any circumstances.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1. Local Storage
All user preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store any personal information on our servers or any external storage.

### 5.2. Security Measures
Since we do not collect any user data, there is no risk of data transmission or storage security breaches. All App functionality is contained within your device.

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1. Access and Control
Since we do not collect any information from you, there is no data to access, correct, or delete.

### 6.2. Data Deletion
All App data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all local data by:
- Uninstalling the App
- Using the "Clear All Settings" option in the App settings

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, children's privacy is inherently protected.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## 9. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

## 10. International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect, store, or transfer any user information, there are no international data transfers associated with the App.
